What this page shows. Verum Signal records specific factual claims attributed to {{ speaker.name }} in ingested articles — claims with concrete, verifiable assertions such as specific figures, named events, or dated facts. Biographical facts, redundant claims, and opinion content are not included.

A verdict on an attributed claim is a statement about the claim's relationship to available evidence — not a characterization of {{ speaker.name }} as a person. These verdicts do not aggregate into a score or judgment about the speaker's overall reliability. Full methodology →

Disagree with a verdict? Every claim and verdict on this page links to its per-article evidence. If you believe a specific verdict was assigned in error, send the verdict ID and the basis for your dispute. Corrections are reviewed under Methodology {{ methodology_version }}.
