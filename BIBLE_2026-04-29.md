# Verum Signal Project Bible — 2026-04-29

## Mission

Verum Signal is an independent claim-analysis platform that helps people think critically across political lines. The system extracts factual claims from news articles, verifies them against independent sources using web search, and publishes per-article scores plus longitudinal outlet reliability scores.

**Core mission language (do not deviate):** Encourage independent thinking, help people question across party lines.

**Brand discipline rules:**
- NEVER use the phrases "fact-check," "fact-checked," "verified" (in fact-checking sense), or "fact verification"
- NEVER position as left or right
- Evaluate claims on evidence, not on who made them
- Methodology v1.5 is the public-facing methodology version

### Methodology v1.5 verdicts (8 total)

Positive direction (counted toward outlet score):
- **Supported** (+1.0): Confirmed by ≥2 genuinely independent Tier-1 sources via different channels
- **Corroborated** (+0.5): Broader pattern of agreement across sources
- **Plausible** (+0.5): Consistent with evidence but only one credible source

Negative direction (counted toward outlet score):
- **Overstated** (-0.5): Real but exaggerated, scaled, or characterized misleadingly
- **Disputed** (-1.0): Credible source contradicts; evidence is mixed
- **Not_supported** (-1.5): Positive evidence contradicts the claim

Excluded from outlet scoring:
- **Not_verifiable** (excl.): Can't be confirmed/denied; primary sources unavailable
- **Opinion** (excl.): Value judgement, prediction, or normative claim that can't be true or false

**Independence rule:** Two sources only count as independent if they obtained information through different means. Multiple outlets repeating the same wire = ONE source.

**Consensus exception:** ≥5 outlets consistently reporting → Supported at confidence 2/3 (even if independent sourcing can't be verified).

**Wire-service exclusion:** AP/Reuters wire copies excluded from per-outlet scoring (the original outlet matters, not the syndication).

## Brand/mission rules (repeated for emphasis)

- Never say "fact-check," "fact-checked," "verified," or "fact verification"
- Never position as left or right
- Evaluate claims on evidence, not on who made them
- Methodology v1.5 evaluates factual claims wherever they appear — including in opinion essays. Per-claim Opinion verdict is for assertions of view (not for any content from /opinion/ URLs)
- The Opinion verdict applies at the per-claim level, not the article level
- Sources must be independent (different channels of obtaining info) to count as separate; wire copies don't count as multiple sources
