CLAIM 4
OVERSTATED · 2/3

The program is projected to save Riverdale County $3.2 million annually once fully implemented.

Core claim is real but framed more confidently than evidence supports.

DISPUTED · 1/4

1) State press release — cites the 38% figure. 2) Peer-reviewed wellness research literature — typical sick-day reductions in similar programs cluster at 8-14%, not 38%. 3) State Auditor preliminary review — questioned the pilot sample size (n=42). 4) Independent reanalysis published by State University researchers — could not replicate the 38% figure using the disclosed pilot data.
