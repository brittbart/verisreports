Privacy Policy

Effective May 5, 2026 · Verum Signal

Verum Signal is a media-credibility tool that helps people evaluate news articles using a published methodology. This policy describes what data the Verum Signal browser extension and website collect, how that data is used, and what choices you have.

What the browser extension does

When you visit a webpage, the Verum Signal extension reads the hostname of that page (for example, foxnews.com) and sends it to our server to look up that outlet's reliability score. We use this score to display a small badge on the page and in the extension popup.

We do not read or transmit:

  • The full URL of the page you're visiting (we only see the hostname)
  • The page's content, headlines, or article body
  • Any text, forms, or input you've entered on the page
  • Cookies, browsing history, or session data from any site
  • Personally identifying information of any kind

If you click "Analyze this article" inside the extension popup, you explicitly send the article URL you're viewing to our server so we can extract and verify its claims. This is the only case in which we receive a full URL, and only after you click the button.

What our website collects

When you visit verumsignal.com or use any of our public web pages (the homepage, outlet pages, leaderboard, or report pages), our server logs standard request information:

  • The page or API endpoint you requested
  • The IP address of your request (used for rate limiting and abuse prevention only)
  • Your browser's user-agent string (e.g., "Chrome on Windows")
  • The HTTP status of the response
  • The time of the request

These logs are kept for operational purposes (debugging, capacity planning, abuse prevention) and are not used to build a profile of you or your browsing behavior. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics trackers, and we do not sell access to logs.

What we do not do

  • We do not run advertising or display ads.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share user data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for commercial purposes.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking. The site may set a cookie for technical session purposes only.
  • We do not require an account or login to use the extension or the public website.
  • We do not collect biometric, financial, health, or location data.

Data retention

Server logs are retained for up to 30 days for operational purposes, after which they are deleted. Article reports you generate by submitting a URL are stored in our database indefinitely as part of the public verification record (since other users may view the same report), but are not associated with you in any way — there is no user account, no session, and no cookie linking the report to your identity.

Children

Verum Signal is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the service, please contact us at the email below and we will assist as needed.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will update the "Effective" date at the top of this page and post a notice on the homepage for a reasonable period of time. We will not retroactively apply less-protective practices to data collected under a prior policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns, contact: privacy@verumsignal.com

For corrections to a verdict or methodology questions: disputes@verumsignal.com
